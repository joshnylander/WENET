WENET Developers Corner

WENET is an open effort to facilitate law and justice data sharing. Developers are core to this effort. Check this page for link to usefull information, contact information, upcomming dates/events and other usefull information for WENET developers.

To assist with developer communication a e-mail distribution list has been created. To ask questions and pose solutions to the group, send your e-mail to "misdevelopers(at)co.whatcom.wa.us".

For project members, you can access the SharePoint site here.
