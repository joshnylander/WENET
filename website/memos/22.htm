Abstract

This document specifies the Whatcom County WENET Cluster. Specifically the cluster participating organizations that are sharing Law and Justice information using the WENET Protocol and the information used to discover their service points.

1. Whatcom County Regional WENET Cluster

The Whatcom County Regional WENET Cluster is a collection of WENET service points that have all met the membership requirements and been approved by the Management Information Systems Committee of the Whatcom County Law and Justice Council. The cluster is identified by the URI: http://www.wcljc.org/wenet/cluster/whatcom/.
